xen/mm: introduce NUMA-aware claim sets

Hi all,

This short series extends Xen's memory claim handling to support claim
sets spanning multiple NUMA nodes, while keeping the existing single-
claim behaviour available for compatibility.

The first patch is a preparatory rename. It splits the domain's existing
outstanding_pages accounting into separate global_claims and node_claims
fields. This makes the accounting model easier to follow ahead of the
functional change, and keeps the hot-path checks efficient by preserving
aggregated claim totals.

The second patch introduces a new domctl for installing a claim set in a
single operation. A claim set may contain one global claim and multiple
node-specific claims, allowing toolstacks to express NUMA-aware memory
reservations atomically. The implementation includes claim validation,
allocation-time claim protection, and claim retirement on success while
retaining the legacy claim interface for the legacy hypercall API.

Patch summary:

1. xen/mm: Split d->outstanding_pages to global_claims & node_claims
2. xen/mm: Introduce Claim Sets for multiple NUMA nodes

Thanks for your review.
